Intersection 01
Heat × Invisible Labour
India's welfare and climate systems depend on labour they refuse to formally recognise.
"A system that relies on women's willingness to work for less than minimum wage is not a welfare programme. It is an extraction model with a development vocabulary."
"India's climate adaptation conversation treats heat as an environmental variable rather than a labour variable. That framing determines who the policy protects."
"The system she helped build cannot account for her departure because it never formally accounted for her presence."
